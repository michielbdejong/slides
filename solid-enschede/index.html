Users love choice? (dilemma)

Centralisation has negative consequences
for innovation and choice.

  • Which integrations do we build?
    • Log in with Facebook everywhere?
  • Developers depend on central platforms
    for data and identity.
    • …or they have to start such a platform.
  • People have lost control over their data
    and can't navigate between services.

Solid decouples data from services.

Solid lets people use services but choose where they store their data.

Users stay in control
of their own data and
choose selectively what to share.

Users decide where they
store each piece of the data.

Users give services access
to specific parts of their data.

Separating data from services
allows permissionless innovation.

Services with decentralized data
require a different approach.
